005 : Greenwood Homes Website.

The main task of the website developed for the Hungarian GreenwoodHomes (ECO LIVING) Construction and Contracting Company was to meet the client’s requirement that the website be fully in sync with the modern and sleek appearance of the premium properties to be built. To achieve this, a thorough blueprint and interior design analysis was conducted prior to development. The result was a responsive HTML5 website, coded with several dynamic elements (JavaScript), that interactively showcases the architectural projects offered by the company to visitors.
