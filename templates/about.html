03 : About me

My name is Benedek Áron Ladányi, and I am a Full Stack and Machine Learning Developer. I currently work at an international company where my primary responsibilities include developing and maintaining corporate websites, optimizing SEO, and creating department-specific applications. My main focus is the implementation of AI and machine learning technologies on a global scale within the company. I primarily work with PHP, Python, and JavaScript in my daily tasks.
I completed my studies in Hungary at the University of Szeged, where I earned MSc degrees in Geography and Geography Teaching. During my university years, I became fascinated with geoinformatics, particularly in areas like GIS (Geographic Information Systems) and satellite remote sensing. Recently,
I have delved deeper into the world of machine learning, exploring the potential of this field, such as model development, predictive systems, data processing, and visualization.
Key technologies I work with:
Python: Flask, TensorFlow, Matplotlib, Conda, Colab, Pandas, Numpy, Scikit-learn, Keras
PHP: Laravel
Other tools: Git, GitHub, Docker, Bitbucket, Jira
I enjoy working in teams, especially when agile methodologies support the process. I believe in hard work, continuous learning, and the importance of integrity.
In my free time, I love coding for personal projects (websites and Python-based applications) and am always eager to learn new things. Additionally, I enjoy swimming, cycling, and attending cultural events.
